I. Introduction

The objectives of PAM V1.1 program are:


  • To collect each semester from the subsidiaries commercial information regarding cards business activity,

  • To manage data on behalf of all BHFM subsidiaries in a centralized and mutualised database ,

  • To release information towards all the BHFM users using reports which comprise tables and graphics.

in order to follow through and monitor cards business activity within BHFM subsidiaries network. This program replaces and follows the EXCEL sheets format used in 2010.


PAM V1.1.1 is available on BHFM intranet network to be used by all BHFM actors involved in cards business. PAM V1.1 is available in two languages: French and English.

Data and reports are dedicated to internal use. They don’t have to be published, transferred out of the bank, except under the agreement of bank’s management.

All users with rights to access this program can see all the reports.

The data base is saved two times a day and is running under a disaster recovery plan.
