II. Data description

PAM V1.1 manages data regarding ATM, POS, Cards and merchants contracts. Users have to make a distinction between stock end flow data:

  • Stock as number of ATM, POS , cards or active contracts at the end of the semester ,

  • Flow as number of transactions occurred during the semester, as accumulate amounts (volume) of the transactions occurred during the semester.

This data and the Excel sheets used on 2010 have been previously defined and validated by BHFM and subsidiaries. They are the basement of cards businesses activity monitoring tools to be used by BHFM.
