3) Merchant contracts portfolio

Each subsidiary has to produce two times a year the number of merchant contracts signed and the number of active merchant contracts on the 30 of June and the 31 of December, dispatched on acquiring channel.

    • Acquiring channel to be specified: ATM ON SITE, ATM OFF SITE, POS TERMINAL OFF BRANCHES and POS TERMINALS ON SITE.

For each channel :

    • Number of merchant contracts signed ,

    • Number of active merchant contracts.
