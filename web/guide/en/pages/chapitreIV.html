IV. Users roles and access rights

There are three users roles defined as following:

    • Application administrator : BHFM/MON/GFL is the only user who has the right of :

      • Managing users and theirs rights,

      • Deleting records,

      • Reading all the data,

      • But no right to capture and update data.

    • Writer in a subsidiary: write and update data of the subsidiary. One or two people in each subsidiary will capture, verify and update information on cards business. The subsidiary is responsible for the quality of its data.

    • Reader : read data of the subsidiary

      • The readers of the subsidiary have the right to consult the data of the subsidiary and all graphics.

      • The BHFM readers have the right to consult the data of all the subsidiaries and all reports.


Anyone in BHFM headquarters and in subsidiaries can apply for the rights of reader. There may be as many readers as necessary to a subsidiary.

Requests for new readers or writers accounts should be sent to the application administrator after being validated by the management of the subsidiary. Email address: Animation.Cards@socgen.com .

There is no DELETE records function available for writers. Only the administrator has the right to delete records in the database. If the removal of records in the subsidiary tables is required, the writer should contact by mail the administrator to perform this operation.
